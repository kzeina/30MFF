about this fridge

what is this?

a digital version of your daily fridge struggle: standing in front of the fridge, door open, staring at the same food you ignored 10 minutes ago, hoping something new magically appeared.

this site is your brutally honest companion for when you're hungry but nothing looks good, thirsty but water seems too boring, and too indecisive to just pick something already.

navigate the tabs to explore your options (or lack thereof). click items to get the judgment you deserve. the fridge doesn't care about your feelings.
